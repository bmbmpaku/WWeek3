The Cookie Jar

This game is simple:
  • Once you hit the start button, you are rewarded with 1 Cookie per click.
  • Your objective is to collect as many cookies into your Cookie Jar without breaking the jar.
  • You can get more cookies every second by buying special toppings.
  • You can increase the size of your cookie jar by trading your toppings.
  • Lastly have fun and dont break the jar, thats how you get ants!!!!!
